Witchers, from the Kingdom of Cintra

A Witcher is someone who has trained mentally and physically for years, learned alchemy, learned monster hunting, and finally mutated through a series of inhumane experiments that make them powerful magic users and assassins.

Although they kill monsters, they are veiwed as bad since they are "mutants" that have the ability to easily kill a person

How do you make a Witcher?

  1. An individual must be recruited
    • You have to be a boy, perferablly orphaned
  2. Be entered into the "Trial of Grasses", which enable them to have supernaturak powers
    • Where boys must consume corn lily, nightshade, speargrass, wildrye, wolfspan and that weaken their immune system
  3. "Trial of Dreams"
    • A series of psychedelic visions induced so they have night vision
  4. "Trial of Mountains"
    • Young boys have to survive in the mountains with their learned knowledge
